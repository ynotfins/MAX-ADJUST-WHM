# PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines the policies of Max Adjust LLC (“Company”) regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information (“Information”) from users of the website www.maxadjust.com (the “Site”) and participants of our text messaging services. By using the Site or opting in to receive text messages, you agree to abide by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

### **Collection of Information**

The Company collects Information through the following means:

1. **Website Use**

    - Forms completed by users on the Site.
    - Information provided through surveys and prize promotion entries.
    - Maintenance and analysis of web server logs.
    - Communication via email and other means between users and the Company.
    - Cookies, single-pixel GIF image files (“web beacons”), and web server log analysis to track site trends and enhance user experience.

2. **Text Messaging Services**

    - Name, phone number, and other information provided when users opt in to receive text messages.

3. **Non-Personally Identifiable Information**

    - The Company may collect non-personally identifiable information through cookies or similar technologies. This may include IP address, referring site, and technical details related to your visit.

4. SMS opt-in and phone numbers collected for SMS communication purposes will not be shared with any third party and affiliates for marketing purposes

### **Types of Information Collected**

-   Name
-   Postal address
-   Email address
-   Telephone number
-   Cell phone number
-   Age and date of birth
-   Gender
-   IP address and referring site
-   Other technical information collected by the Site’s servers

### **Use of Information**

The Company uses Information for the following purposes:

1. **General Use**

    - To provide requested information, products, and services.
    - To allow users to participate in surveys, promotions, and receive relevant updates.
    - To enhance the user experience and improve Site functionality.
    - To deliver marketing communications and offers from the Company or selected third parties.

2. **Text Messaging Services**
    - To send text messages with updates, promotions, and relevant information about products and services.

### **Protection of Information**

The Company takes reasonable measures to safeguard your Information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no security measures can provide absolute protection.

### **Sharing of Information**

1. **General Sharing**

    - The Company may share Information with third-party contractors for services such as site operation, communication, order fulfillment, and information verification.
    - Third parties may use cookies or similar technologies in advertising on the Site.

2. **Text Messaging Services**
    - The Company may share your Information with third-party service providers that help deliver text messages.
    - Information will not be sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

### **Opting Out**

1. **General Marketing Communications**

    - Users can opt out of receiving future offers by following instructions within marketing communications or contacting the Company.

2. **Text Messaging Services**
    - Users can opt out of receiving text messages by following instructions provided in the messages or contacting the Company directly.

Opting out of Company communications does not apply to communications from third parties, whose privacy policies govern their use of Information.

### **Children’s Privacy**

The Site and text messaging services are not intended for users under 18. The Company does not knowingly collect Information from children under 13.

### **Your New Jersey Privacy Rights**

Under New Jersey Civil Code Section 1798.83, residents of New Jersey may request information regarding the Company’s disclosure of personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please contact the Company.

### **Changes to this Privacy Policy**

The Company reserves the right to revise this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting to the Site or notification through text messaging services. Continued use of the Site or services constitutes acceptance of updated policies.

**Contact Information**  
For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
